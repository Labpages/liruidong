关于我 / About Me

李瑞东，1978年5月生，正高级工程师，泰山产业领军人才。曾荣获山东省技术发明奖一等奖1项，中国电子学会科技进步奖二等奖1项、山东电子学会科学技术奖一等奖1项。

Rui Dong Li, born in May 1978, is a Senior Engineer and a Taishan Industrial Leading Talent. He has won the First Prize of Shandong Province's Technology Invention Award, the Second Prize of the China Electronics Society's Science and Technology Progress Award, and the First Prize of the Shandong Electronics Society's Science and Technology Award.
